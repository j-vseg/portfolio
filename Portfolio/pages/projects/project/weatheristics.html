Weatheristics

PWA | Progressive Web Application

Check the weather with Weatheristics

Weatheristics is a weather app. It helps you to be more aware of your surroundings regarding the weather. It sends push-notifications when bad weather is arriving to make you are well prepared when leaving your home or know when to take shelter when on a run.

Check out code

Client: Fontys Hogeschool

Duration project: 4 weeks

Features

  • Retrieve weather information from your location
  • Retrieve weather information from a city
  • Send push-notifications by bad weather nearing
